Why the Wake Shad?
Not all baits work well over thick vegetation, but the Wake Shad is built for it. It doesn’t run more than three inches deep, You can reel it right through that crisp, green hydrilla, keeping it just above the grass.
This ability to skim the surface while creating enticing movement is what makes it so effective. It mimics a wounded baitfish, and when burned over grass flats, it triggers aggressive strikes.
VanDam isn’t just fishing for fun, he’s fishing to win. “I want to win every tournament I enter,” he says. “I don’t compete for accomplishments. I do it because I love it, and it’s fun.
