TITLE: Jacob Wheeler Shares his Reel Ratio Strategies | Bass Angler Magazine

Okuma and Touring bass professional Jacob Wheeler gives us a little insight on how to utilize the different gear ratios for different applications. Why choose a Okuma Helios 6.6:1 for spinnerbait, chatterbait or swimbait. Jacob explains that the Helios 7.3:1 is a good all around reel and the right choice is you can only have one ratio. The Helios 8.1:1 reel is good for flipping, frogs and getting fish out of cover fast.
