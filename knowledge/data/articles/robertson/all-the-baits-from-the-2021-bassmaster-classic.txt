TITLE: ALL the baits from the 2021 Bassmaster Classic!

Just about everyone flipped bushes in 2-4′. Some hit other wood too, many said the bushes had to be low and green, some said bushes with grass or on points/bumps were better. Bottom line was almost everyone fished the EXACT SAME WAY as everyone else, all day long. One of the most insane things I’ve ever seen in a tournament. Anyhow, here’s the bait rundown for the full field:
1: Hank Cherry
Hank’s full fishing details are in his winning pattern post here. Here’s his 2 main baits:
> Jerkbait: New Berkley Stunna (stealth shad), “straight out of the pack, you don’t need to change the hooks. I was limited on the colors I had [because the bait’s not out yet] but since I’ve had ’em I’ve just had a lot of success with that color…always a good one to throw around shad spawns.
> Jerkbait gear: 6′ 10” M mod Abu Garcia Winch Rod, Abu Garcia Zata Reel, 15-lb Berkley Trilene 100% Fluorocarbon.
> Jig: 1/2-oz Picasso Hank Cherry Straight Shooter Jig (gp magic?) with various trailers including the new/not yet out Berkley MaxScent Chigger Craw (pumpkin green flake). “I literally would put different trailers on it to make me think I was throwing a different bait.”
> Trailers were gp/blue, pumpkin/green, sometimes he added a little chartreuse on the tips – to imitate bluegills.
2: Matt Arey

> Frogs: Lunkerhunt Compact Frog (white), popping-style frog (bluegill), 50-lb P-Line TCB Braid, same reel, 7′ 3″ H Lew’s Greg Hackney Frog Rod [believe it’s Academy only]. Around a variety of flooded grass in 1-2′.
