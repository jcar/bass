TITLE: A Better Way to Fish Senkos Faster

Gary Yamamoto shared his simple and effective system for rigging and fishing the famed Yamamoto Senko for bass years ago with Jason Sealock. A Senko rigged on a weighted screw lock hook allows you to cover water much quicker than wacky rigging or fishing a weightless Senko, while still maintaining excellent skipping attributes. Jason takes us through the rigging process and how he fishes this bait in different scenarios.
