Everybody knows that the good ol’ Yamamoto Senko is one of the most prolific fish catchers ever created, but Alabama pro Fletcher Shryock depends on a different bait when he’s fishing pressured bass and around lighter cover – the Gary Yamamoto PsychoDad.
